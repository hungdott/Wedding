Our Love Story

Our first met

Jul 31, 2021

We first met online via zoom meeting because of covid pandemic. We watched the movie "Nhắm mắt thấy mùa hè" which had a meaningful message and gave a deep touch to our hearts. "If one day you can not see, what do you want to see the most?"; "If one day you can not hear, what do you want to hear the most?";... What a highly recommended film.

We fell in love

Oct 23, 2021

After about three months, we could finally meet in person at Hoan Kiem lake. I came late 45 mins but Mr. Bear still patiently waited for me. He gave me a bunch of roses and I held them nicely while walking along the road. We talked a lot, then he drove home with me. I had given him a piece of cake before he drove back home.

Our first trip

Jul 18, 2022

Hoi An retreat - Da Nang - Hue. We traveled together for the first time to some beautiful places. We had the chance to explore things and try new things together. We swam in the sea, did yoga in the morning, ate avegan diet, drew our dream, and enjoy the breathtaking scenery. Especially, Mr.Bear organized a warm party for my birthday with my friends there. I was very surprised and happy.

Our Engagement

Feb 12, 2023

The moment when seeing the groom's family coming to my house my heart beat faster. We felt happy and had the first experience like a celebrity. All people looked at us and asked to take photos together. Our engagement was organized successfully by our super parents with lots of support from our relatives. We felt so grateful at each moment.
